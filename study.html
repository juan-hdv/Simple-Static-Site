Study

Study is one of the most rewarding activities. When you learn a new art or a new subject, that knowledge remains for life. Give yourself the opportunity to study something you are passionate about in your spare time.

Below are three ideas of resource for personal study:

Moocs

A massive open online course (MOOC /muːk/) is an online course aimed at unlimited participation and open access via the web. There are many of them and most of them are excelent:

Alison, Canvas Network, FutureLearn, Kadenze, Khan Academy, Linkedin Learning, Open Universities Australia, OpenClassrooms, openHPI, OpenLearning, OpenSAP, Peer to Peer University, POLHN, Shaw Academy, Stanford Online, SWAYAM, The Great Courses, WizIQ.

If you want you can study languajes, arts, music, history, computing, economy, laws, etc. You can try some of the following:

  • Coursera
  • edX
  • iversity
  • Udacity
  • Udemy
  • Udacity
  • Udemy

     



Youtube

Youtube is also a good source for short tutorials and courses on diversity of arts and everyday problems.

Books and learning Apps

Do not miss the opportunity to find what you like, as there are many applications in the Android Store and Apple store to read books, study topics, listen to books, listen to podcasts, etc.
